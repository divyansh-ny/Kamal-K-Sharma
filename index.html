"The Piggy Bank Billionaire" is a Financial Guide to help individuals understand their financial goals in a most prudent manner and plan them with utmost seriousness. There is lot of confusion in every investor's mind on what to buy & how much is sufficient. This book will work as a personal guide for people in all age groups, this will help individuals to understand Why, How & Where of their financial goals. The language in book has been kept very simple to benefit all and make it easy to understand.

About the Author

Kamal K Sharma is Founder Director at www.savefirst.co.in . After an excellent career spanning over two decades he initiated his dream project SaveFirst Investinsure Services Pvt Ltd in 2016 which remains an amazing platform to spread financial literacy among masses. He has worked in various roles, capacities and across different territories. His career span of 23 years has enabled him working across industries including Office Automation & Financial sector.

His last assignment has been with International Netherland Group (ING) in Life Insurance vertical where he worked as General Manager for over 10 years. Kamal has wide travel experience & travelled across UK, Australlia, South Africa, Europe, Hong Kong, Dubai and Sri Lanka. He specializes is Defence Sales & has conducted over 300 plus IAPs (Investor Awareness Program) & Financial Literacy Workshops for Indian Army, BSF & CRPF.
